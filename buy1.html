The Tragic Prelude

by John Steuart Curry

"The Tragic Prelude" by John Steuart Curry depicts the abolitionist John Brown and the events surrounding him. The painting portrays Brown as a strong and determined figure, symbolizing his righteous conviction and unwavering commitment to ending slavery. It captures the intensity of his beliefs and actions, highlighting the tragic consequences of his failed raid on Harpers Ferry and his significance in the lead-up to the Civil War.
